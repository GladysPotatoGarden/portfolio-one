# Quincy Zientek — Portfolio

A portfolio site that introduces me and what I’ve built.

## Why this website is unique

**Soft landing** - When you open the page, the background settles in first; then each section fades in from top to bottom.

**Easy to move around** - The menu jumps straight to “About” or “Projects” with a smooth glide instead of snapping.

**Projects that read clearly** - Each piece of work gets a card with a clear label and a screenshot.

**Looks right on your phone** - Headlines stay centered and readable when the screen gets narrow.

**Little polish** - Added favicon, footer tucked at the bottom, and a color palette that stays consistent.
