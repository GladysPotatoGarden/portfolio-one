# Quincy Zientek — Portfolio

A calm, dark-themed site that introduces who you are and what you’ve built—without feeling busy or loud.

## What makes it feel good

**Soft landing** — When you open the page, the background settles in first; then each section fades in from top to bottom, like lights coming up on a stage. Quick enough that nobody’s waiting around.

**Easy to move around** — The menu jumps straight to “About” or “Projects” with a smooth glide instead of a jarring snap. Tap the name in the corner anytime to float back to the top.

**Projects that read clearly** — Each piece of work gets a card with a clear label and a screenshot, so visitors see what you mean at a glance.

**Looks right on your phone** — Headlines stay centered and readable when the screen gets narrow—no awkward wrapping or tiny walls of text.

**Little polish** — Same vibe in the browser tab (favicon), footer tucked at the bottom, and a color palette that stays consistent from the nav to the footer.

*Built to be skimmed, remembered, and easy to share.*
